# Report Contract

## Purpose
Freeze the minimal reporting surface so future scopes do not drift into ad hoc notes.

## Already Known
- `master_report.md` is the governance surface for current status and next scope.
- Each closed scope must produce one explicit report under `reports/`.
- Reports must state trusted inputs, verified results, blockers, and the next recommendation.

## Intentionally Unbound In RN1
- Exact long-term template for execution-heavy versus design-heavy scopes.
- Whether lab-generated operator reports later get their own contract family.
- Any future machine-readable report index.
