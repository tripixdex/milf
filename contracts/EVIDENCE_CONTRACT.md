# Evidence Contract

## Purpose
Define what counts as canonical tracked execution evidence versus local-only runtime residue.

## Already Known
- Markdown reports under `reports/` are canonical.
- Reviewed screenshots under `artifacts/` are canonical when they support a closed scope.
- Scope reports must separate observed facts from inferred or unverified statements.

## Intentionally Unbound In RN1
- Final naming convention for future evidence bundles.
- Whether manifests or machine-readable indexes will be introduced later.
- Whether raw Windows dumps/logs are ever promoted into canonical tracked evidence.
